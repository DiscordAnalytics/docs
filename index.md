# What is Discord Analytics?

![Discord Analytics Dashboard](https://r2.discordanalytics.xyz/images/home/header_image_dark.webp){.dark-only}
![Discord Analytics Dashboard](https://r2.discordanalytics.xyz/images/home/header_image_light.webp){.light-only}

Discord Analytics is a powerful, free-to-use monitoring and statistics platform built for Discord bot developers. It allows you to track, visualize, and understand your bot's activity in depth, providing the insights you need to grow and improve your project.

Discord Analytics provides a centralized dashboard to see exactly how users are interacting with your creation, and how your bot is growing over the time.

## Core Features

- **Interactive & Custom Graphs**: Track key metrics like user growth, command usage, and server activity through dozens of customizable graphs tailored to your bot's specific needs.
- **Collaborative Workspaces**: Work as a team by easily inviting your developers and moderators to access your bot's statistics together.
- **Unified Vote Webhooks**: Streamline your integrations by merging all your bot-list vote webhooks into one single, manageable endpoint.
- **Automated Weekly Reports**: Stay updated without opening the dashboard by getting a comprehensive summary of your bot's performance delivered straight to your email inbox every Sunday.
- **Custom Achievements**: Engage your users by crafting unique milestones to flex, or grab fan-favorite achievements directly from the Community Store.
- **Free & Community-Driven**: Discord Analytics is completely free to use and transparently funded by community donations.
